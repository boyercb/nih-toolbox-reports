Patient Reported Outcomes Measurement Information System (PROMIS) is a system of highly reliable, valid, flexible, precise, and responsive assessment tools that measure patient-reported health status over the past 7 days.

A higher PROMIS T-score represents more of the concept being measured. For negatively worded concepts like anger, a T-score of 60 is one SD worse than average.
